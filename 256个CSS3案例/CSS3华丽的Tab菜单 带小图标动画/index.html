Space-Saving Navtabs with Icons – in SVG

These SVG navtabs combine the concept behind Douglas Bowman’s “Sliding doors”, and SVG template technique with SVG icons. The tabs require two SVG shapes: one very wide one for the left side, and one small one, just for the right corner.
